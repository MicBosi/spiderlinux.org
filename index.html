Download Spider Linux

Latest version: Spider Linux 1.0 32 bits (Based on Linux Mint 17.3)
Note: you will see references to Linux Mint and Ubuntu in the installer, feel free to ignore them.

Usage

  • Installation: at boot when asked select the first option (GRUB GUI).
  • You can develop your HTML, CSS, JS and PHP files on /var/www/html.
  • To see your website simply point your browser to 'http://localhost'.
  • To open phpMyAdmin simply point your browser to 'http://localhost/phpmyadmin'.
  • MySQL and phpMyAdmin passwords: 123456.
